Export Terms

Everything you need to place your first order with GEEI.

📦

Packaging

Export-grade PP and jute bags, 5 to 25 kg, machine stitched with inner liner. Private label and custom packing available on request.

🚚

Minimum Order

One 20 ft container, approximately 13 to 14 MT depending on variety and stem option. Mixed-variety containers supported.

⚓

Ports of Loading

Chennai, Krishnapatnam and Visakhapatnam, direct from the Guntur belt. CIF or FOB as per buyer preference.

💳

Payment

Irrevocable LC at sight or advance TT. Terms for repeat buyers discussed on relationship basis.

📈

Pricing

Quoted weekly against Guntur market rates for your grade and quantity. Request a live FOB quote below.

🔍

Samples and Inspection

Pre-shipment samples on request. Third party inspection welcome at buyer's option before container loading.
